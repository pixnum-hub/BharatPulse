ℹ️ About & Data Methodology

BharatPulse is an independent, unofficial project with no affiliation to, endorsement by, or sponsorship from the Government of India or any of its ministries, departments, or agencies.

Most figures on this page are not live feeds. They are mathematical estimates: a published baseline figure from a credible source (RBI, NPCI, Ministry of Railways, DataReportal, etc., cited in each section's footnote) is extrapolated forward every second using its known annual growth rate or fiscal-year pace — the same technique used by public "population clock" style sites. They approximate real-world totals but are not official real-time statistics and will not exactly match any government dashboard at a given instant.

Static facts (geography, national symbols, country profile) are drawn from public sources including the free REST Countries API and general reference knowledge, and are believed accurate as of mid-2026 but not independently verified against primary government sources.

This app is provided for informational and illustrative purposes only. Please verify any figure against its original source before relying on it for research, reporting, or decision-making.
